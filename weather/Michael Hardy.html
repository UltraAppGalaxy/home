Idea For The Making : I got the idea to make this App after my Mom said she needed an advanced Real Time Weather App that would have alerts and etc.. . After exploring the Web I couldn't find one that contained Real Time Alerts So I decided to Create the most advanced Weather App Available For Android Devices and It's Called " Ultra Weather App! "
About The Author:: My name is Michael Hardy and I'm the Author of Ultra Weather App. I enjoy Walking, Camping, Movies and etc... I have been Programming since 1989 and started creating Software on an Apple IIe in 1989. In 1994 I begin to develop Software and Game's For Windows. Programming is my main hobby and I do it mainly for fun however, I do have Some Published Titles that I sell through Pay-Pal and other Distributors.
Development Tools Used : I Created The Ultra Weather App Using The Android Studio Development Environment On Microsoft Windows! After I Compiled and Built the App I then Used APK Editor Pro On Android To Tweak The App and Made Some Minor Changes. For The Graphics Contained Within The App I Used PicsArt Pro and PhotoRoom Pro Two Graphic App's On Android... For the Launcher Icons I used an Online Android Launcher Icon Generator...
